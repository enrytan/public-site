01 / ABOUT

I'm a full stack developer based in Batam, Indonesia. For most of the last decade I've worked remotely for international clients, usually as part of teams spread across the US and India, so working across time zones and coordinating handoffs is just normal to me.

My background is backend first. I'm most at home in C#, ASP.NET Core, and SQL heavy systems, and that's still where I think most naturally. I'm a generalist. I like owning a system end to end rather than staying in one lane, and I've grown comfortable being the person who picks up the unfamiliar part and makes it work. I've also mentored other developers and helped set code standards along the way.
